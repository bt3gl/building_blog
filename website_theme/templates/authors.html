Hello World


I am Dr. Marina von Steinkirch, but you can call me Mia. I am a former Theoretical Physicist turned into Machine Learning // Infrastructure // Software Engineer.

I have been in love with Science, Hacking, AI, and the Mysteries of the Universe since middle school. Then I went to DEF CON and fell in love with hacking this Matrix. I presented a talk on Quantum ♄ Cryptography at the Cryptovillage once.

Stop for a second, feel your breath traveling your body, look around and realize the place you are - as an entity with consciousness, and perhaps free will - located at this very moment. We are all alive inside this astonishing World, full of complicated rules, and intricate beings. What are we all doing here? After wandering for many years trying to find the Meaning of Life, I realized that it's simply staying where reality is and be your best. So, overall, over this timeline constantly moving forward (which we can't control), my purpose in this game is to connect Science + Technology + Business Intelligence to solve impactful and meaningful problems for humanity and our society.


Life as a Software Engineer:


  • Many Hats Engineer at Reaction Commerce, where I help developing full-stack & infrastructure e-commerce solutions (Node.js, React, GraphQL, Kafka, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, CircleCI, Docker, Kustomize, Kubernetes, AWS EKS, CDK, CloudFormation, Terraform, GitOps/Flux).

  • Senior Software Engineer at Surfline, where I built an end-to-end pipeline & software so that surfers can retrieve clips of the waves they caught, from over 500+ cameras worldwide (Python, AWS Lambda, SQS, SNS, Terraform, MongoDB, Jenkins). I helped to maintain a worldwide cameras infrastructure (AWS, Docker, Wowza Streaming Engine, Kubernetes, Skaffold, Golang).

  • Senior Infrastructure Software Engineer at Etsy, where I developed an incident response infrastructure using OSQuery to retrieve Etsy's fleet data (with customized MySQL queries) and Elastalert for alerting, inspecting and detecting security anomalies in both Etsy's corp and engineering fleet (Python, Docker, Elasticsearch, Chef, Jenkins, GCP). I wrote a security suite for GCP, that identifies and alerts on possible security flaws and misconfigurations.

I helped writing Apple's Deep Learning toolkit for richer data augmentation for image recognition (CUDA, C++).

  • Security Software Engineer at Yelp, where I created an in-house machine learning software for DNS/Web endpoint data analysis (Python, Scikit-learn, MongoDB, AWS, Docker). I and worked on several infrastructure and security tasks (Chef, Jenkins, AWS forensics, web security).

I modeled a high-dimensional Game of Life using deep learning to learn advanced rules and statistics. I developed Python libraries/Jupyter notebooks for their quantum gravity theory. I taught Agile, JIRA, and DevOps practices for a team of 10+ scientists.
