I research Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Security at Apple. Additionally, I am starting as a machine learning researcher collaborator at the Caltech HEP Lab.

Formerly: I was a software engineer at Yelp, I spent some softwaring retreat time at Recurse Center, and I was a Physics PhD Researcher at LANL, BNL, and NASA.

I believe our generation can radically change the world to make it fairer. The insurgent A.I. revolution have the potential to help humanity, specially low-resource communities, to enjoy their time in this Universe with more dignity and opportunities.

Partially because of my background in Sciences, I am a strong believer that A.I. helpers are going to open an entire new chapter in the search of new Physics and in the paradigm of how we understand the Universe.

Beyond Computers and Science, I am fond of shredding on a board, existentialist science fiction, art, photography, Cinema, playing the Blues in my guitar, and figuring out how to make the future better.
