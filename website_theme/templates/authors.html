Hi there!



<3 #1: Science & Computers

I research Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Security at Apple. Because science is fun, I am a machine learning collaborator at the Caltech HEP Lab.

I am passionate about the applications of Artificial Intelligence in the perceptions of the human condition: being it in Security, Arts, Music, Science, Photography, or Telling Stories.

Formerly, I was a software engineer at Yelp, I spent some softwaring retreat time at Recurse Center, and I was an Astrophysicist PhD Researcher at LANL, BNL, and NASA.



<3 #2: Cinema, Photography & Fiction

Back from my times as an Astrophysicist, I have been using pixels as a form of expression. Now, in my free time, I make films at the San Francisco School of Digital Filmmaking.

Photography has always been a gig in my life, great part with film cameras. You can check my artistic work here.

I love reading existentialist science fiction, and sometimes I try to write some pieces.



<3 #3: Adrenaline, Adventures & Appreciating the World

My family is originally from Italy and Germany but I grew up in a small town in Brazil. Because of my studies and my PhD, I have lived in several places in the world, including: Sao Paulo, Chile, Boston, Washington DC, New York City, and San Francisco.

These experiences gave me the sense of how beautiful humankind is. We are all unique in our particular features, but very similar in our search to understand our presence in this reality.

I am crazy about new adventures, very fond of shredding on a (surf/skate/snow)-board, hiking around the world and climbing, and skydiving.



You own your life and you can be whoever your heart wants! Love!
