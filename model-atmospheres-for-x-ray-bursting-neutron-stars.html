Model Atmospheres for X-ray Bursting Neutron Stars

Thu 01 December 2016 Category Astrophysics

My last paper from my PhD research together with the Los Alamos National Laboratory was finally published in the Astrophysical Journal!

We show results of spectral analysis of simulations of Neutron Star's atmospheres, comparing with data from objects known as X-Ray bursters.
